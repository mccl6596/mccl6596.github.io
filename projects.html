Fragment Shaders with WebGL: May 2022
This in-class project, Computer Graphics, was given in 2022. As it was the last project of the semester, this project was given to have fun with shading. The website includes the same gif with different styling:

  • Normal
  • Black & White
  • Fish Eye
  • Ripple
  • Toonify
  • Custom

My custom shader, featured in the picture, was done to attempt a metallic look. It took all colors of the gif and brought the darker colors to a light grey, and lighter colors to a dark grey. The in-between colors were placed in the middle of the two.
